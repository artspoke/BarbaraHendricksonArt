About the Artist

Barbara Hendrickson is a contemporary artist whose work explores the interplay of light, color, and emotion. Her paintings invite viewers to pause and reflect, finding beauty in both the familiar and the unexpected.

Working primarily in oils, Barbara creates pieces that range from intimate still lifes to expansive landscapes, each infused with her distinctive vision and attention to detail.
