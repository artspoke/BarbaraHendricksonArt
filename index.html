About the Artist

I am Barbara Hendrickson, born Barbara Julie Applezweig. I was educated at The High School of Music and Art where I majored in Art. Both my mother and father painted, mostly my mother, who was a serious artist. For my seventh birthday I got an oil painting set. I started painting in earnest in 1980 when we moved into a house where I had a room with great light where I could see the colors bounce off my paintings. I started painting on large canvases where my mostly figurative paintings were just about life size. As I finished them I began to think of them as my friends. Buyers may feel that way as well, at least I hope they do. I have also done some abstract paintings.
